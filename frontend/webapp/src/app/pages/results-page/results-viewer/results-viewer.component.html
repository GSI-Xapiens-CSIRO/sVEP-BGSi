Column Definitions

Column Description
Rank Ranks or order number
. Undefined
Region Genomic region with chromosome, start, and end positions
Alt Allele The altered allele
Consequence The outcome or result of a genetic variation
Gene Name The name of the gene
Gene ID The identifier for the gene
Feature Feature as reported by GTF file (column 3)
Transcript ID & Version The identifier for the transcript along with its version
Transcript Biotype The biotype of the transcript
Exon Number The number of the exon
Amino Acid Change The change in amino acid if any
Codon Change The change in codon if any
Strand Which DNA strand (+/-)
Transcript Support Level The support level for the transcript, indicating the quality of the evidence supporting the existence of the transcript.

Consequence Explanations

Term Description
missense_variant A type of genetic mutation where one DNA base pair is changed, resulting in the replacement of one amino acid with another in the produced protein.
splice_region_variant A variation in the DNA sequence near or within the splicing regions of the gene which may affect the accuracy of splicing and thus lead to an altered gene product.
non_coding_transcript_exon_variant A variation occurring within non-coding exons of a transcribed gene. Non-coding exons may contain regulatory regions critical for protein synthesis.
upstream_gene_variant A variation located upstream (5 prime direction) of the transcription start site of a gene, this could have potential impacts on gene regulation and expression.
NMD_transcript_variant A variant which is predicted to undergo nonsense-mediated mRNA decay (NMD). When such a variant occurs in a coding sequence, it can prevent the production of functional proteins.
